Workshop 2026

18th annual workshop and conference on computational mathematics and modelling (WOCCOM2026)

Date: 29 June – 03 July 2026

Venue: University of KwaZulu-Natal, Pietermaritzburg

Theme: Introducing Climate Models, Block Hybrid Methods for Boundary Value Problems and Effective Use of Large Language Models in Numerical Approximations.

Eligibility Criteria: Please note that abstract submissions and participation are specifically restricted to applicants affiliated with institutions in Sub-Saharan Africa and the UK.
